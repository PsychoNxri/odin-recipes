Healthy Teriyaki Salmon Salad

A healthy option for the evening!

This healhy teriyaki salmon rice salad will make sure you get your omega 3 intake as well as keeping you fuller for longer with the nutty brown rice.

Again, this is a super easy recipe and one that I love to eat on a Sunday evening with my girlfriend.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked brown rice (from 1 cup uncooked rice)
  • Salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Sesame seeds
  • Asparagus
  • Store bought Teriyaki sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 C
  2. Grease a baking dish with a little bit of olive oil and add the salmon fillets.
  3. Pour sauce over the salmon fillets and marinate for at least 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the rice in a pot of boiling water for 30mins
  5. While rice is cooking, steam asparagus and set aside
  6. Bake the salmon fillets for 13 or until the salmon is cooked through. Cooking time depends on the thickness of the salmon.
  7. Once rice is ready, transfer to a bowl and lay asparagus ontop
  8. When the salmon is ready, add some sesame seeds on top, garnish with chopped chives and serve immediately.
